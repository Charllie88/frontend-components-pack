# Tutor Landing Page

## Project Overview

A professional landing page designed for a tutoring service, showcasing educational expertise and personalized learning solutions. The page emphasizes credibility, subject expertise, and student success stories.

## Target Audience

- Parents seeking academic support for their children
- High school and college students needing subject-specific help
- Adult learners preparing for standardized tests
- Students struggling with specific subjects or test preparation

## Key Features

- **Hero Section**: Compelling introduction with key statistics and clear call-to-action
- **About Section**: Professional background and teaching philosophy
- **Services Section**: Detailed breakdown of tutoring subjects and pricing
- **Testimonials**: Authentic student and parent feedback
- **Contact Section**: Multiple contact methods and consultation booking
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for all devices with mobile-first approach

## Services Offered

- **Mathematics**: Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Statistics ($45/hour)
- **Science**: Biology, Chemistry, Physics ($50/hour)
- **English & Writing**: Grammar, Literature, Essay Writing ($40/hour)
- **Foreign Languages**: Spanish, French, German, ESL ($42/hour)
- **Test Preparation**: SAT, ACT, GRE, TOEFL ($55/hour)
- **Study Skills**: Time Management, Organization ($38/hour)
